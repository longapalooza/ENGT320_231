07.Example 1

Given and Required:

Using the energy balance method, derive the finite-difference equation for the ($ m $, $ n $) nodal point located on a plane, insulated surface of a medium with uniform heat generation.

Solution:

A sketch of the system is shown below.

Applying the energy balance to the control volume of size $ \frac{\Delta x}{2} \cdot \Delta y \cdot 1$ shows
$ q_1 + q_2 + q_3 + q_4 + \dot{q}\left( \frac{\Delta x}{2} \cdot \Delta y \cdot 1 \right) = 0 $
Begining with $ q_1 $, it may be seen that this is from node ($ m - 1 $, $ n $) to node ($ m $, $ n $). This arrangement is simple conduction. Thus
$ q_1 = k \left( \Delta y \cdot 1 \right) \frac{T_{m - 1, n} - T_{m, n}}{\Delta x} $
The heat $ q_2 $ goes between nodes ($ m $, $ n - 1 $) and ($ m $, $ n $). This arrangement is again simple conduction. Thus,
$ q_2 = k \left( \frac{\Delta x}{2} \cdot 1 \right) \frac{T_{m, n - 1} - T_{m, n}}{\Delta y} $
The heat $ q_3 $ is on the insulated surface (i.e., it's an adiabatic surface) and thus is by definition equal to zero (i.e., $ q_3 = 0 $). Finally, the heat $ q_4 $ is between nodes ($ m $, $ n + 1 $) and ($ m $, $ n $). This arrangment is once again simple conduction so
$ q_4 = k \left( \frac{\Delta x}{2} \cdot 1 \right) \frac{T_{m, n + 1} - T_{m, n}}{\Delta y} $
Combining all of these expressions into the energy balance expression yields
$ k \left( \Delta y \cdot 1 \right) \frac{T_{m - 1, n} - T_{m, n}}{\Delta x} + k \left( \frac{\Delta x}{2} \cdot 1 \right) \frac{T_{m, n - 1} - T_{m, n}}{\Delta y} + 0 + k \left( \frac{\Delta x}{2} \cdot 1 \right) \frac{T_{m, n + 1} - T_{m, n}}{\Delta y} + \dot{q}\left( \frac{\Delta x}{2} \cdot \Delta y \cdot 1 \right) = 0 $
Since $ \Delta y = \Delta x $, the above expression may be simplified to
$ k \left( T_{m - 1, n} - T_{m, n} \right) + \frac{k}{2} \left( T_{m, n - 1} - T_{m, n} \right) + \frac{k}{2} \left( T_{m, n + 1} - T_{m, n} \right) + \frac{\dot{q}}{2} \left( \Delta x \right)^2 = 0 $
Dividing everything by $ \frac{k}{2} $ shows
$ 2 \left( T_{m - 1, n} - T_{m, n} \right) + T_{m, n - 1} - T_{m, n} + T_{m, n + 1} - T_{m, n} + \frac{\dot{q}}{k} \left( \Delta x \right)^2 = 0 $
Collecting like terms yields the final solution

$ 2 T_{m - 1, n} + T_{m, n - 1} + T_{m, n + 1} - 4 T_{m, n} + \frac{\dot{q}}{k} \left( \Delta x \right)^2 = 0 $
